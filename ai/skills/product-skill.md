# Product Skill

## Product
EdgeAlertReceiver is an iOS companion app that connects to Garmin devices through Connect IQ and rings a loud bell on iPhone when the CIQ app sends an alert message.

## Primary User Value
- Fast, reliable pedestrian warning from phone audio.
- Minimal setup friction.

## Definition of Done
- Device connects through Garmin Connect Mobile device selection flow.
- App receives messages (`receivedMessage`) reliably.
- Bell alert plays immediately and clearly.
- Relevant logs are emitted for diagnosis.
